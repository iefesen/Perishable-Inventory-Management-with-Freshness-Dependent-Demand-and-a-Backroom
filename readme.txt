------Perishable Inventory Management with Freshness Dependent Demand and Backroom---------
***********
This repository contains the optimization, heuristic solutions, and the output for various experiments for the paper titled
"Perishable Inventory Management under Freshness-Dependent Demand and Backroom Effect" by Ilgın Efe Şenyuva, Melvin Drent, and Zümbül Atan. You can find the full paper in:
https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5056130
***********
